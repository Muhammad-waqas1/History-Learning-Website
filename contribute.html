How to Contribute

We welcome contributions from developers, historians, and enthusiasts worldwide. Here’s how you can get started:

  • Fork the repository on GitHub.
  • Make your changes or add new features.
  • Submit a pull request with a detailed explanation.
  • Collaborate with our team to review and merge your changes.

Why Contribute?

Learn and Grow

Contributing to our project provides a platform to learn new skills, explore historical topics, and grow as a developer or researcher.

Make an Impact

Your work can help create a comprehensive resource for history enthusiasts and students across the globe.

Community Guidelines

We value respect, inclusivity, and constructive feedback. When contributing, please adhere to our Code of Conduct to maintain a welcoming environment for everyone.
